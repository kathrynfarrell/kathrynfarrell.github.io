§ 1: About me

I'm a senior undergraduate in the Neuroscience and Statistics & Machine Learning departments at Princeton University. I'm passionate about reconstructing the brain's algorithms with deep neural networks, statistics, and dynamical systems, and believe that there are are extremely exciting discoveries to be made at the intersection of neuroscientific and machine learning models.
